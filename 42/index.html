An HStack with an H1 and two HStacks nested inside. HStacks that are direct children of other HStacks have display: contents. This is because HStacks with Spacers expand a lot and shrink, but then HStacks without Spacers are forced to shrink, too — and we don’t want them to shrink if the only have content and no Spacers.
