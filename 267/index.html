The white boxes are anchor elements. You can scroll horizontally with the Magic Remote by clicking the red area and moving the remote sideways. But you can click and drag the anchor element.

Ideally, I’d expect an experience similar to touch devices like iOS: you can scroll horizontally by placing the finger anywhere—including anchor elements—but you can also tap and hold (long press) on an anchor element to copy it, drag and drop it, etc.
